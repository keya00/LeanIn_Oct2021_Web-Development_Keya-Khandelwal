TOP CAFES NEAR YOU IN DELHI

Though these aren’t all, we’ve tried to fit into this list as many options as we could. There is an infinite number of best cafes in Delhi that you must check out when touring the country’s capital. Check out the following list to know better about the famous cafes in Delhi, not just the famous cafes the list will also take you to some of the best nearby places to visit in Delhi. So read on and don’t forget to including the following in your Delhi itinerary.




Top choices


AMA Cafe

In the narrow lanes of Majnu Ka Tila, AMA Cafe is defo one of the most highly recommended eateries. Popular for its luscious cheesecakes and crusty pizzas, this place will offer you a gorgeous spread.



  • Address: House 6, New Colony, Majnu Ka Tila
  • Phone Number: +91 11 6610 3668
  • Cost For Two: ₹550 for two people (approx.)

Cafe Wink

With many cafes popping up in East Delhi, what sets this one apart is its modern furniture and eye-popping decor. The drool-worthy food and their thick yummy shakes will make your taste buds ask for more. Their Kit Kat Shake and Brownie Shake are to die for, so go drop by for your share!



  • Address: Plot 9, Shreshtha Vihar, Anand Vihar
  • Phone Number: +91 8130 470 123
  • Cost For Two: ₹800 for two people (approx.)

The Big Chill Cafe

The OG destination serving Italian yummies, this cafe does not even need an introduction now, does it Delhiites? With the peppiest vintage ambience we’ve ever seen, The Big Chill Cafe has been our go-to hangout spot since it popped up all those years ago.



  • Address: Khan Market, CP, DLF Mall Of India & Other Outlets
  • Phone Number: +91 11 4175 7533
  • Cost For Two: ₹1500 for two people (approx.)

Diggin

One of the prettiest places in the city, Diggin’s a place known to be all romantic and Insta-worthy. Serving Italian and Continental cuisines, we love this place for its food! These lovelies also have a separate Winter Menu, serving yummies like Plum Pudding served with Brandy Sauce and Mushroom and Truffle Crostini. Apart from this, you should also sample their Penne Picante, Porky Pizza and Apple Crumble Pie fosho!



  • Address: CP, Chanakyapuri & Anand Lok
  • Phone Number: +91 11 4080 0082
  • Cost For Two: ₹1400 for two people (approx.)

Ricos

Pampering us with tons of all kinds of yummy dishes, ranging from Mexican, Italian and Mexican, Ricos is one of our hiding holes in the city. They have super-cool wooden decor and graffiti which is just so Insta-worthy! When at Ricos, gorge on their Mushroom Cheese Burst, Chicken Florentine, The Farmer John Burger and Vegetarian Lasagna, ‘cause you’ll defo love them!



  • Address: Kamla Nagar & GTB Nagar
  • Phone Number: +91 11 6517 0044
  • Cost For Two: ₹1400 for two people (approx.)

Rose Cafe

An Instagram dream come true, this pretty and pastel-imbued cafe . Once you make your way in, the space does beautifully for a long, winter reading session with a cup of French Pressed and Pesto Chicken waffles, or a summer afternoon with an Iced Chamomile & Mint Basil Tea.

  • Address: 2, Westend Marg, Saidulajab, Saket
  • Phone Number: +91 9810088762
  • Cost For Two: ₹1,000 for two people (approx.)
